A minimalist, e-ink friendly launcher designed specifically for the Mudita
Kompakt and other e-ink devices.

Key features:
* Mudita Integration: Full support for system apps from Mudita Kompakt.
* E-Ink Optimized: High-contrast design with hollow-dot status icons.
* Focus-Oriented Navigation: Minimalist app drawer and favorite apps.
* Privacy First: No personal data ever leaves your device.
